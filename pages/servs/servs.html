Service One

Our team of dedicated volunteers is passionate about turtle conservation and is committed to making a difference in the lives of these amazing animals. We work with local communities, schools, and government agencies to promote turtle conservation and raise awareness about the importance of protecting these vulnerable creatures.

Service Two

At our rescue center, we provide a safe and nurturing environment for turtles of all species, including those that have been injured, sick, or abandoned. Our goal is to provide them with the care and support they need to recover and thrive, so that they can be released back into their natural habitats.
